About
The Tournament

APL is Ashoka University's annual intra-college football tournament — three days of competitive, mixed-gender football bringing together undergraduate and postgraduate students every Spring Semester.

Now in its ninth edition, APL has grown into more than a tournament. It's where campus community meets sporting spirit — with group stages, knockouts, live commentary, and a whole lot of pitch-side energy.

Whether you play, watch, or cheer from the stands, APL is for everyone who loves the game.
